About me

Hi! I am Ph.D. Candidate at the University of Pennsylvania and advised by Pratik Chaudhari. I work on a handbook for pre-training models. My research interests span areas like multi-task learning, transfer learning, unsupervised learning and information theory. I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2017 with a Dual Degree (Bachelor in Mathematics and Pgysics), where I was advised by Yaokun Wu.
