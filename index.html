About me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the University of Pennsylvania and advised by Pratik Chaudhari. I work on a handbook for pre-training models. My research interests span areas like multi-task learning, transfer learning, unsupervised learning and information theory. I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2017 with a Dual Degree (Bachelor in Mathematics and Physics), where I was advised by Yaokun Wu.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Multi-task Learning, Transfer Learning and Unsupervised Learning

  • Deep Gnerative Models

  • Information Geometry
